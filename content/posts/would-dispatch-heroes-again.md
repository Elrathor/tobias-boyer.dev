The 10 hours over the last couple of weeks were the shortest 10 hours I may have ever spent. Where my last game, Bloodlines 2, was an ugly puppy that you love anyway, [Dispatch](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2592160/Dispatch/) is a beautiful golden retriever who is objectively the very, very best boy.

This game has a well-written story, great humor, gorgeous animations, and great voice work, and it's all bundled up with a sweet but addictive gameplay loop. It even nailed the story elements I was most excited about.

Wish I could say more about it, but then I'd have to dispatch a couple of heroes in your direction to make sure you forget who I was talking about. So, better I keep it zipped.

Let me make this review short and sweet: I give the game 10 out of 10 Astro Pulses. Will 100% play again.

Enough yapping from me. Go get it for yourself! You won't regret it.
